# Legal Compliance Audit Report
## Website: frickeldave.de
## Date: March 19, 2026
## Auditor: German Legal Compliance Manager

## Summary
⚠ Issues Found

The website has a generally solid foundation for legal compliance under German law but is missing some critical elements required by law. Key legal documents are present but incomplete, and there are missing mandatory declarations.

## Critical Issues

### 1. Missing Accessibility Statement (Barrierefreiheitserklärung)
- **Legal Requirement**: Required under BITV 2.0 (Barrierefreie-Informationstechnik-Verordnung) for public sector websites and strongly recommended for commercial sites
- **Current Status**: No accessibility statement found anywhere on the website
- **Impact**: Non-compliance with accessibility requirements under German disability discrimination law
- **Remediation**: Create and implement a detailed accessibility statement conforming to WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards

### 2. Missing Image Attribution/Copyright Notice (Bildnachweis)
- **Legal Requirement**: Required under UrhG (German Copyright Act) for all used images
- **Current Status**: Images are used throughout the site (especially in handmade product gallery) without visible attribution or licensing information
- **Impact**: Potential copyright infringement claims
- **Remediation**: Implement a comprehensive image attribution system with clear licensing information

## Warnings

### 3. Incomplete Impressum Information
While the Impressum contains basic required information, it is missing some specific details:
- No registered business number (Handelsregisternummer) if applicable
- No chamber membership information (IHK/Municipal authority) if applicable
- No complete professional liability insurance details if required for business activities

### 4. Outdated Template Content in Terms Page
- **Issue**: The terms page contains template content referencing "Jane Doe" instead of the actual website owner
- **Impact**: Unprofessional appearance and potential legal ambiguity
- **Remediation**: Update terms content with accurate information about Frickeldave

### 5. Missing Specific Consumer Rights Information
- **Legal Requirement**: For e-commerce sites, detailed consumer rights information is required under German Civil Code (BGB)
- **Current Status**: Basic information present but could be expanded for comprehensive compliance
- **Impact**: Potential consumer protection violations
- **Remediation**: Add detailed consumer rights section including withdrawal policy, warranty information, shipping costs, delivery times

## Recommendations

### 6. Enhanced Privacy Policy Improvements
While the privacy policy is quite comprehensive, improvements can be made:
- Add cookie consent banner implementation details
- Clarify data processing timeline details
- Include information about automated decision-making if applicable

### 7. Professional Dispute Resolution Information
- Implement information about participation in dispute resolution proceedings as required by § 36 VSBG
- Add links to online dispute resolution platform (EU ODR)

### 8. Age Verification and Youth Protection
- If the site serves content for children or teenagers, implement age verification where necessary
- Add youth protection information where required

### 9. Enhanced SEO Legal Markup
- Implement structured data markup for legal pages to improve discoverability
- Add canonical URLs to prevent duplicate content issues
